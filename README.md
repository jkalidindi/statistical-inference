# Statistical Inference

Hypothesis testing and inferential statistics case studies using t-tests, ANOVA, chi-square, and A/B testing.

## Projects

### E-News Express — A/B Testing
**Problem:** An online news portal wants to test whether a new landing page drives higher user engagement and subscription conversions than the existing page.  
**Methods:** Two-sample t-test, chi-square test, A/B test design  
**Files:** `ENews_Express_AB_Testing_Notebook.ipynb`, `ENews_Express_AB_Testing_Solution.ipynb`

---

### Chi-Square Test of Independence
**Problem:** Determine whether two categorical variables are statistically independent using real business datasets.  
**Methods:** Chi-square test of independence, contingency tables  
**File:** `Chi_Square_Independence_Test.ipynb`

---

### Common Statistical Tests
**Problem:** Practice exercises covering the most commonly used statistical tests in business analytics.  
**Methods:** z-test, t-test, chi-square, ANOVA  
**File:** `Common_Statistical_Tests.ipynb`

---

### Medicon — Inferential Statistics
**Problem:** A pharmaceutical company needs to draw conclusions about drug effectiveness from sample data.  
**Methods:** Confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, one-sample and two-sample tests  
**File:** `Medicon_Inferential_Statistics.ipynb`

---

### Diet Study — ANOVA
**Problem:** Compare the effectiveness of multiple diet plans on weight loss across groups.  
**Methods:** One-way ANOVA, post-hoc tests  
**File:** `Diet_ANOVA_Case_Study.ipynb`

---

### Microsoft Stock Price Analysis
**Problem:** Analyze Microsoft stock price data to identify trends and test statistical properties of financial time series.  
**Methods:** Descriptive statistics, normality tests, time series EDA  
**File:** `Microsoft_Stock_Price_Analysis.ipynb`

---

### Titan Insurance — Statistical Tests
**Problem:** Use statistical tests to draw business insights from insurance customer data.  
**Methods:** Hypothesis testing, t-tests, chi-square  
**File:** `Titan_Insurance_Statistical_Tests.ipynb`

---

### Talent Hunt — Hypothesis Testing
**Problem:** Analyze examination data to evaluate student performance and test hypotheses about score distributions.  
**Methods:** Hypothesis testing, ANOVA, t-tests  
**File:** `Talent_Hunt_Hypothesis_Testing.ipynb`

---
